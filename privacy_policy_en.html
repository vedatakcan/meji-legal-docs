Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Meji (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles your information when you use our mobile application, Meji (the “Application”). By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact details.

All gameplay progress, scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device via a Room database. This data is never uploaded to any server and cannot be accessed, restored, or viewed by the Developer.

We may collect limited non-personal information automatically for functionality, analytics, advertising, and app performance purposes, including:

  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, performance data, and other app performance metrics collected to improve app stability and performance.
  • App Activity: User interactions within the app (e.g., buttons clicked, screens viewed) to help improve app functionality and experience.
  • Device or other IDs: Device model, operating system version, language settings, and advertising ID used by Google AdMob for personalized and non-personalized ads.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, app interactions, and advertising data. This data is shared with AdMob for app functionality, analytics, and advertising purposes. Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing – Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment information is handled exclusively by Google and is never accessible to the Developer. Google Play Billing Privacy & Security Information

The data types collected and shared by the app for these purposes include:

  • Diagnostics: Collected and shared for app functionality, analytics, and advertising or marketing.
  • Other app performance data: Collected and shared for app functionality, analytics, and advertising or marketing.
  • App interactions: Collected to monitor and improve app experience.
  • Device or other IDs: Collected and shared for app functionality, analytics, and advertising or marketing.

3. Data Security

Your gameplay data is stored securely on your device only. If you delete or reinstall the Application, or if your device malfunctions, your data may be permanently lost. The Developer cannot retrieve, recover, or restore your local data under any circumstances.

Any data collected by third-party providers (such as AdMob or Google Play Billing) is handled by them in accordance with their own security and privacy practices.

4. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the Application or manually delete app data.
  • Third-Party Data: Retained according to the policies of the respective third-party service providers.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application is suitable for general audiences and does not contain harmful or inappropriate content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or applicable local age thresholds). Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise usage.
